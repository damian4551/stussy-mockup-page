history

In the late ’80s and early ’90s, a brand was born from the Southern California surf scene and swept through the clothing landscape to redefine the look and ideology of casualwear. That brand was Stüssy, a label that grew organically from youth movements and inadvertently revolutionized the clothing business.

stüssy/gore-tex

Stüssy and GORE-TEX® have made another collection of garments ready to shield you from the elements. Using patented GORE-TEX® breathable waterproof fabric to create a co-branded, shell, pants, and bucket hat. Staying dry is easier than drying out after you’re wet.
stüssy/birkenstock
Photography: Liam MacRae Model: Laurie Faso Mei
Stüssy has collaborated with quintessential German footwear brand Birkenstock on the iconic Boston silhouette. Corduroy debossed suede in two colorways, bone and anthracite, both feature the signature anatomically designed footbed, dark brown suede lining, and the Stüssy logo debossed on the outside upper. Slip-on and slide away.

Stüssy and Nike are back for the second round of infinitely wearable laid-back silhouettes. From Douglas Firs to Palm Trees, co-branded modern staples activate the imagination and bring a positive vibe.
